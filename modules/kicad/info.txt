KiCad Electronic Design Automation Suite
Version: 9.0.1 (or latest available for your distribution)
Description: KiCad is an open source software suite for Electronic Design Automation (EDA). It facilitates the design of schematics for electronic circuits and their conversion to PCB designs.
Dependencies: None
Maintainer: Strix OS Team
Website: https://www.kicad.org/
Notes: Installation method varies by distribution. For Ubuntu, uses official PPAs. For other systems, may use native package manager or Flatpak.
